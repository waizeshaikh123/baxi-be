Privacy Policy

At Baxify, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers’ personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our postage label service.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy sets out the basis on which we collect and use personal information from our customers. By using our postage label service, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Introduction

We Collect We may collect the following types of personal information from you when you use our postage label service:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Payment information (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
  • Shipping history and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information We use your personal information to:

  • Process your shipping labels
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our service
  • Communicate with you about new features and promotional offers
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with our trusted third-party partners who assist us in providing our services, such as:

  • Shipping carriers (UPS, FedEx, etc.)
  • Payment processors (PayPal, Stripe, etc.)
  • Customer service providers
  • Marketing partners

5. Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure connections (HTTPS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Access controls
  • Firewalls
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and to comply with legal obligations. If you wish to have your information removed from our systems, please contact us at report@baxify.com

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website and to understand how our service is being used. Cookies are small files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to remember your preferences and to provide a more personalized experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these websites before providing them with your personal information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

10. Your Rights Under applicable data protection laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Correct or update your personal information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Restrict the processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal information
